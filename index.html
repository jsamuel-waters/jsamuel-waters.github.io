Cool Bands

  1. Get Bread, Mustard, Salami, and Provolone cheese
  2. Lay out two slices of bread
  3. Spread mustard onto the face up side of each slice
  4. On one bread slice, on top of the mustard, layer slices of salami and provolone at a 3:1 ratio, or as desired
  5. Place the other bread slice mustard down on top of the layers of salami and cheese
